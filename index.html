The History of Football

The Invention of Football

Football, known as soccer in some countries, evolved from various ball games played around the world. The modern rules of football were established in the mid-19th century, with England's Football Association codifying the game in 1863.

The First World Cup

The first FIFA World Cup was held in Uruguay in 1930. It featured 13 teams, and Uruguay emerged as the inaugural champions. The event marked the beginning of the world's most prestigious football tournament.
